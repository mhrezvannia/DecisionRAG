# Quarterly Decision Support Pilot

The Applied Intelligence Lab ran a twelve-week pilot to evaluate whether retrieval-augmented assistance could reduce analyst review time on internal decision documents. The pilot corpus included project reports, policy notes, and technical memos from the Decision Systems group.

The pilot was led by the Applied Intelligence Lab, with operations support from the Decision Systems group. Analysts used retrieved passages to inspect prior decisions before drafting a recommendation.

A central outcome from the pilot was lower review time. Median analyst review time decreased from 18 minutes to 11 minutes after retrieval assistance was introduced.

The team also observed that ambiguous questions such as "what happened?" or "what about the result?" often required a follow-up question before a trustworthy answer could be produced.

Policy Note: Evidence-Grounded Decision Support

The review board requires generated explanations to cite at least one supporting source from the document set.

If evidence is conflicting, too thin, or only weakly related to the question, the system should abstain rather than speculate.

For employee-facing or policy-sensitive decisions, human oversight remains required for moderate-confidence outcomes.

Clarification should be requested when the user question does not identify the relevant program, time period, section, or metric.
